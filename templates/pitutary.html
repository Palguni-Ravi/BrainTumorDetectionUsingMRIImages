Treatment Options

  • Medications: Some pituitary tumors can be managed with medication that helps regulate hormone levels and control tumor growth.
  • Surgery: Surgical removal of the tumor may be necessary, especially if the tumor is causing significant hormonal imbalances or compressing nearby structures.
  • Radiation therapy: Radiation may be used to treat pituitary tumors that are not fully removed surgically or in cases of tumor recurrence.
